- 💕Contribution
  + We propose Target Similarity Tuning for selecting few-shot examples based on the similarity of the programs they describe, improving relevance and downstream performance.
  + We introduce completion engines as an abstraction that can implement rich classes of semantic program constraints, as we demonstrate in SQL, Vega-Lite and SMCalFlow.
  + We introduce a general, constraint-observing decoding algorithm, which aligns programming language constraints with the language model’s token vocabulary.
  + We evaluate our method in three natural language-to-code tasks. CSD and TST both show strong complementary gains in output validity and prediction accuracy across domains.
