## 🎃Proposed Model

- Model Name: `LaMDA`[^lamda]

- 🏠Author Affiliation: `Google`
- Architecture: `Transformer-based neural networks (decoder)`
- Pre-Traing Corpus: The pre-training dataset consists of 2.97B documents, 1.12B dialogs, and 13.39B dialog utterances, for a total of 1.56T words.

- Public Item: `None`
